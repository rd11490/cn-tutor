## GRAMMAR: 不过 (but / however)

This is a **conversational connector** you hear all the time in casual Chinese, and it's slightly different from 但是 or 可是.

**Basic meaning:** "but / however / yet"

**When to use it:**
- **但是 / 可是** = direct contradiction (more blunt)
- **不过** = mild objection / concession (more conversational, softer)

**Compare:**

| Sentence | Meaning | Feel |
|----------|---------|------|
| 我想去，**但是**我没有时间。| I want to go, **but** I have no time. | Direct contradiction |
| 我想去，**不过**我现在有点儿忙。| I want to go, **however** I'm a bit busy now. | Softer, conversational |
| 这个电影很好看，**可是**它很长。| This movie is great, **but** it's long. | Factual contradiction |
| 这个电影很好看，**不过**有点儿无聊。| This movie is good, **though** it's a bit boring. | Opinion, casual |

---

**Key difference:** 不过 is lighter, more conversational. It's often used to agree first, then add a small objection. You see it constantly in natural speech.
