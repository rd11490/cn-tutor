## 听说 vs 以为 (2026-04-24)
听说 = "I heard that / it's said that" — reports information from an external source. 以为 = "I mistakenly thought / I assumed" — expresses a wrong assumption or belief. Use 听说 when citing something you heard; use 以为 for mistaken beliefs.

*Example:* 小林听说那个地方很拥挤。(He heard that place is crowded.) vs 我以为你想去。(I mistakenly thought you wanted to go.)

## 本来...但是 (originally... but) with continuous action (2026-04-24)
本来 expresses an original plan/expectation. 但是 contrasts it with what actually happened. When describing continuous action that went on longer than planned, use 一直 + verb + 到 + [endpoint]. This captures both the duration and the surprise/regret that something went on too long.

*Example:* 我本来早睡，但是我一直看电视到很晚。(I was supposed to sleep early, but I kept watching TV until it was very late.)
